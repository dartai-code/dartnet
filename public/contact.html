Frequently Asked Questions

How do I earn points?

Play mini games, complete daily tasks, spin the wheel, and refer friends. Each activity rewards points to your wallet.

What are campaign rewards?

Special challenges in games that reward $3–$5 per game per month. Clear the target (e.g., Level 30 in Shatter) and earn bonus rewards. Campaigns reset monthly on the 1st.

How do champion prizes work?

Run For Life ($100) and Snake Evolve ($50) have limited champion slots. The first players to hit the target claim the prize permanently. Check each game for remaining slots.

My points didn't save. What do I do?

Make sure you're logged in and have a stable internet connection. If points are missing, email us at dartaioffcial2@gmail.com with your username and we'll investigate.

Can I have multiple accounts?

No. Multiple accounts are against our Terms & Conditions and may result in a ban.
